Odin's Pizza

Description

This pizza is the best in town and it only takes you 10 minutes.
It's so unbelievable tasty.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • basil
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • pepper and salt

Steps

  1. Roll each section into a 12-inch disc
  2. Preheat the oven to about 400°F, or about 200°C.
  3. Bake each pizza for about 10 minutes
